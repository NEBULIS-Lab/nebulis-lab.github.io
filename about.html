About NEBULIS Lab

NEBULIS Lab explores the convergence of distributed systems, GPU scheduling, and embodied intelligence. We aim to design scalable, efficient, and cooperative architectures for the next generation of intelligent systems, where computation is no longer confined to the cloud, but dynamically orchestrated across heterogeneous, embodied agents.

From dynamic GPU orchestration to real-world robot autonomy, our mission is to push the frontier of systems for embodied AI. The name NEBULIS comes from 'Nebula' + 'Intelligence Systems', symbolizing a future constellation of embodied intelligent systems filled with uncertainty and potential.

Our Vision

To become a leading research laboratory in the intersection of embodied intelligence and distributed systems, establishing innovative system architectures that lay the foundation for next-generation intelligent systems.
