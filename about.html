About NEBULIS Lab

NEBULIS Lab is a cutting-edge AI research laboratory dedicated to building reliable, explainable, and deployable artificial intelligence systems for real-world applications.

Our mission is to advance the frontiers of AI through innovative research in embodied intelligence, distributed systems, and marine robotics, while fostering collaboration with academic and industry partners worldwide.

Our Vision

To become a globally leading AI research laboratory, making breakthrough contributions that shape the future of artificial intelligence.
