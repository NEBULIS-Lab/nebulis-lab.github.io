About the Laboratory

NEBULIS Lab is a laboratory dedicated to cutting-edge scientific research. We focus on the development and application of innovative technologies, committed to solving complex real-world problems.

Our team consists of excellent researchers from different fields, working together to advance science and technology, contributing to the progress of human society.

The laboratory is equipped with advanced facilities and abundant resources, providing researchers with an excellent scientific research environment.
